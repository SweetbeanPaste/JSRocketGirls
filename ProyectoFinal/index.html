Lore...

You have find yourself in another dimension! A wasteland of sorts, it is plagued with strange entities HIGHLY hostily. In order to stay safe you must play your card wisely, and let the entities destroy themselves instead of putting your skin at risk.

Be smart! read the characters strenghts and weaknessess, plan a winning deck and survive!

Game Rules

  1. Player can choose 3 card out of the 6 characters
  2. You may delete a card from your deck by clicking on it
  3. Player can modify character stats (although that is cheating)
  4. Once you prepared your deck click on "Battle"! Shall the strongest WIN
